# 📊 FMCG Sales & Performance Analytics (Excel MIS Project)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing sales and performance data of an FMCG company using Microsoft Excel. The objective was to transform raw transactional data into meaningful insights through structured MIS reports.

The analysis covers **2 years of data (FY2022-23 & FY2023-24)** and provides actionable insights for business decision-making.

---

## 🏢 Company Background
**SunRise Consumer Goods Pvt. Ltd.** is a mid-sized FMCG company operating across:
- **Categories**: Personal Care, Household, Food & Beverage  
- **Regions**: North, South, East, West  
- **Channels**: General Trade, Modern Trade, E-Commerce  

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
The company lacked a structured reporting system, leading to:
- No visibility on customer growth/decline
- No regional target tracking
- No product-level profitability insights
- No monthly P&L tracking
- No channel performance comparison

---

## 🎯 Objective
To build **5 structured MIS reports in Excel** to enable:
- Data-driven decision making  
- Performance tracking  
- Business insights generation  

---

## 📂 Dataset Overview
- **Total Orders**: 9,651  
- **Time Period**: April 2022 – March 2024  
- **Customers**: 20  
- **Products**: 25  
- **Total Revenue**: ₹18.49 Crore  

---

## 📊 Reports Created

### 1️⃣ Customer Net Sales Performance
- YoY growth analysis of customers  
- Identified top growing & declining accounts  

**Key Insights:**
- Overall growth: **+4.6%**
- Fastest growing: *Reliance Retail*
- Declining: *DMart, Spencer’s*

---

### 2️⃣ Region vs Target Performance
- Monthly performance vs targets  

**Key Insights:**
- Top region: **North**
- Weak region: **East**
- Best month: **March 2024**

---

### 3️⃣ Monthly P&L Summary
- Gross Sales, Discounts, Net Sales, COGS, Profit  

**Key Insights:**
- Gross Profit: **₹3.85 Cr**
- Discounts: **~6.6%**
- Strongest month: **March**

---

### 4️⃣ Top 10 Product Performance
- Revenue contribution by products  

**Key Insights:**
- Top product: **SunGold Refined Oil 5L**
- Top 10 contribute: **65.7% of revenue**

---

### 5️⃣ Channel Performance Analysis
- Comparison of sales, growth, and discounts  

**Key Insights:**
- Best channel: **General Trade (51% revenue, highest growth)**
- Least efficient: **E-Commerce (highest discounts)**

---

## 📈 Key Business Insights
- Company growing steadily at **4.6% YoY**
- Heavy dependency on top customers and products  
- Strong shift towards **General Trade channel**
- Regional imbalance (North vs East)
- High discounting impacts profitability in E-Commerce
